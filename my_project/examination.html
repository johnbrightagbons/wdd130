Examination

We at Unibright know the importance of examination and as such have structured our examination to help us:
1. Evaluate and grade our learners
2. Motivate our learners to study
3. Add variety to the learner study
4. Identify weakness and correct them
5. Get feedback on our lessons
6. Provide statistics for the lesson and school
7. Accredit qualified students

How do we decide what to test and how to test it?

Our overall examination is consistent with your learning outcomes for the course outline. There are a number of ways we review skills and concepts taught in our course outline. We could:
Use the topics list provided in the course outline
Review your lecture notes to find major concepts
Review the chapter headings and subheadings in the assigned readings

What are the qualities of our exam?

1. Our exam affords all our students an equal opportunity to totally show their learning: We normally reflect on the nature and parameters of our exam.
2. Consistency: If students sit for the same exam twice, they should get a similar grade each time.
3. Validity: We make sure our questions address what we want to evaluate.
4. Use of multiple question types: Different students are better at different types of questions. In order to have all the students show their abilities, exams questions are varied to multiple questions, theory, practical etc.
5. Free of bias: Since our students are from all over the world with different socio-economic background, religion, language, political background etc, we try to eliminate anything that make serve as an obstacle to their students exams.
6. Timely: Our exams spread throughout the term with students given adequate time to apply the feedbacks left by the instructors.
